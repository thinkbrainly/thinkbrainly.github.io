AREA

=========================================

Father bought a plywood for making a new table. If the plywood length is 243 cm and a width of 121 cm, what is the area of the plywood?

__________________________________

The formula in getting the AREA is A=lw

[tex] \tt{A=lw}[/tex]

Now we had to get the area of the plywood.

[tex] \tt{A= ?} \\ \tt{l = 243} \\ \tt{w = 121}[/tex]

----------

[tex] \tt{243 \times 121 = \green{29 \: 403}}[/tex]

[tex] \huge{ \boxed{ \boxed{ \purple{29 \: 403 \: cm}}}}[/tex]

Therefore, the area of the plywood is 29 403 cm.

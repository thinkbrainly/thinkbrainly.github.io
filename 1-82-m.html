28. What is the perimeter of a trapezoidal garden with bass 35 m and 17 m respectively and 15 meters on each side?A. 82 mB. 72 mC. 102 mD. 92 m​


Trapezoid Perimeter

P = a + b + c + d

P = 35m + 17m + 15m + 15m

P = 65m + 17m

P = 82m

The answer is A

✏️PERIMETER

[tex]\blue{••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••}[/tex]

[tex] \boxed{ \sf{Answer:}}[/tex]

A. 82 m

[tex]\blue{••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••}[/tex]

[tex] \boxed{ \sf{Solution:}}[/tex]

To get the perimeter of the trapezoidal garden, we will use the formula of perimeter of trapezoid.

[tex] \sf{P}[/tex] = [tex] \sf{b_{1} + b_{2} + s_{1} + s_{2}}[/tex]

Where:

P - represents the perimeter

b - represents the base

s - represents the side

Given:

[tex] \sf{b_{1}}[/tex] = 35 m

[tex] \sf{b_{2}}[/tex] = 17 m

[tex] \sf{s_{1}}[/tex] = 15 m

[tex] \sf{s_{2}}[/tex] = 15 m

Substitute the givens on the formula.

[tex] \sf{P}[/tex] = [tex] \sf{35\:m + 17\:m + 15\:m + 15\:m}[/tex]

[tex] \sf{P}[/tex] = [tex] \sf{82\:m}[/tex]

Therefore, the perimeter of the trapezoidal garden is 82 m.

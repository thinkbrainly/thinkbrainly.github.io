no

Explanation:

Philippine Area of Responsibility (PAR) lies in:

- 25 degrees North to 120 degrees East

- 25 degrees North to 135 degrees East

- 5 degrees North to 115 degrees East

- 15 degrees North to 115 degrees East, and

- 21 degrees North to 120 degrees East.

So, if a typhoon is located at 19 degrees North and 117 degrees East, it’s not within PAR.

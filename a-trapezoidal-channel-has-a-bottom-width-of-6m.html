30. A wooden cabinet needs repainting.How much surface area needs to be painted if the box has a length of 8m, width of 5m and height of 6m?​


Answer:

To find the surface area of the wooden cabinet:

- Determine the six sides of the box: top, bottom, front, back, left, and right.

- Calculate the area of each side by multiplying the length and width of the box together:

Top and bottom: 8m x 5m = 40 square meters (x2 since there are two of them)

Front and back: 6m x 5m = 30 square meters (x2 since there are two of them)

Left and right: 8m x 6m = 48 square meters (x2 since there are two of them).

- Add up the six areas to get the total surface area:

2(40) + 2(30) + 2(48) = 80 + 60 + 96 = 236 square meters.

Therefore, the total surface area that needs to be painted is 236 square meters.

26. What is the purpose of a product key or serial number during the installation process? A. to activate softwareB. to upgrade the softwareC. to uninstall the softwareD. to repair the software(prove ur answer)​


Answer Approved:

A. The purpose of a product key or serial number during the installation process is to activate software. This is often required to ensure that a user has a legitimate copy of the software and to prevent piracy. Without a valid product key or serial number, the user will not be able to activate the software and therefore will not be able to use it.

Explanation:

Additionally, the activation process may also involve registering the software with the manufacturer, which can provide additional benefits such as access to updates, technical support, and other resources. Product keys or serial numbers are unique identifiers that are usually printed on the packaging or provided in an email or digital download. Users must enter this key accurately during the installation process to activate the software and start using it.

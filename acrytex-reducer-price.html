3. country. How longA tablet's price was reduced from P45,000.00 to P33,500.00. Find thepercent reduction in price.Rubrics:​


DECREASE

==================================

Problem

A tablet's price was reduced from P45,000.00 to P33,500.00. Find the percent reduction in price.

Solution

Determine the reduction percent using the formula given

[tex]\bold{||\:Percent_{Decrease} = \frac{Old\:Value\:-\:New\:Value}{Old\:Value}}[/tex]

[tex]\large\rm{\red{Percent_{Decrease}} = \frac{45, 000-33, 500}{45, 000} }[/tex]

Calculate the decrease percent by finding out the difference between the numbers where we subtract the old value by the new value, after that we divide this 'difference' we got to the old value.

[tex]\large\rm{Percent_{Decrease} = \frac{45, 000-33, 500}{45, 000} }[/tex][tex]\large\rm{Percent_{Decrease} = \frac{11,500}{45, 000}}[/tex][tex]\large\rm{Percent_{Decrease} = 0.25}[/tex]

Now that we got the 'decrease' multiply it by a hundred.

[tex]\large\rm{Percent_{Decrease} = (\red{0.25}) (100) }[/tex][tex]\large\rm\red{Percent_{Decrease} = 25\% }[/tex]

[tex]\therefore[/tex] The percent reduction in price is [tex]\boxed{\red{25\%}}[/tex]

==================================

19. a tablet was reduced from 25,000 to 19,500. Find the percent reduction in price. Expressed your answer in decimal ​


PROBLEM:

A tablet was reduced from 25,000 to 19,500. Find the percent reduction in price. Expressed your answer in decimal form.

Original Amount: 25,000

New Amount: 19,500

Step-by-step explanation:

Percent Reduction Formula: (Original Amount - New Amount)/Original Amount) x 100 or (25,000 - 19,500)/25,000) x 100

Subtract the final amount from the initial amount to find the amount of the reduction.

To perform these methods, we need to identify the initial and final values.

Final Amount: 19,500

Initial Amount: 25,000

25,000 - 19,500 = 5500

To find the rate of reduction, divide the amount of the reduction by the original amount.

5500/25,000 = 0.22

Multiply the rate of reduction by 100

0.22 x 100 = 22%  

The Answer is 22%

Percent Reduction in Price: 22%

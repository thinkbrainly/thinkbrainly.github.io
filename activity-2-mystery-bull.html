7. A farmer rears a total of 875 bulls and chicken. The number of chicken is 24 times the number of bulls. How many bulls are there in farm?


Answer:

There are 35 bulls in the farm.

Step-by-step explanation:

Word Problem

Let us analyze the given problem above.

What is asked?

The number of bulls in the farm.

What are the given?

total number of bulls and chickens - 875

number of chickens - 24 times the number of bulls

What is the equation?

Let x be the number of bulls.

24x be the number of chickens.

x + 24x = 875

Solution:

x + 24x = 875

25x = 875

25x/25 = 875/25

x = 35

Final Answer:

There are 35 bulls in the farm.

Checking:

x + 24x = 875

35 + 24(35) = 875

35 + 840 = 875

875 = 875 ✔

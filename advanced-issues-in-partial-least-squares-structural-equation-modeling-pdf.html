Two types of earthquake locationHypocenter - below the Earth's surfaceEpicenter - above the Earth's surface

Layers of Earth

The Earth has 4 layers, namely:

Inner coreOuter coreMantle Crust

When these layers keep moving towards each other by bumping or sliding past each layer, Earthquakes occur. Sometimes, earthquakes tend to be strong in magnitude, thus, causes high intensity damages to infrastractures and buildings. Higher magnitude of earthquakes also causes landslides and tsunamis, thus, it is important that proper measures are observed.

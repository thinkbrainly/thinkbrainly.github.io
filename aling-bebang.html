15. 1.Inday and bebang are comparing their heights.if Inday's height is 120¾cm and bebang's height is 96⅓cm.what is the difference in their heights​


Answer:

Asked: It was asked the difference of the height of Inday and Bebang.

Given: Inday's height was 120 ¾ cm while Bebang's height was 96 ⅓ cm.

Operation: Subtraction

Number Sentence: 120 ¾-96 ⅓=N

Step-by-step explanation:

Solution:

120 ¾-96 ⅓

=120 9/12-96 4/12

=24 5/12

Complete Answer: The difference in their heights was 24 5/12 cm.

Checking:

1.

24 5/12+96 ⅓

=24 5/12+96 4/12

=120 9/12

=120 ¾

2.

120 ¾-24 5/12

=120 9/12-24 5/12

=96 4/12

=96 ⅓

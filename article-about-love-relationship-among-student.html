14. describe one relationship that exists among living things ​


Answer:

Parasitism: Symbiotic relationship in which one organism lives in or on another organism (the host) and harms the host. Mutualism: Symbiotic relationship in which both species benefit from the relationship. Commensalism: Symbiotic relationship in which one organism benefits and the other is not helped or harmed

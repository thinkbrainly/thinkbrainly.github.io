17. 8192 is what term of 2,16,128...?


Geometric Sequence Formula:

An = A1(r)^n-1

An = 8192

A1 = 2

r = 16/2

r = 8

Solve for nth term:

Substitute values

8192 = 2 (8)^n-1

Divide both sides by 2

4096 = 8^n-1

Convert both sides into logarithmic expression

log8 (4096) = log8(n-1)

log8 (8^4) = log8(n-1)

4 log8(8) = log8(n-1)

4 = n-1

Transpause

5 = n or n = 5

Answer: 8192 is the 5th term

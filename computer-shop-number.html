11. 1. A computer shop charges P15.00 in every hour of computer rental. Represent yourcomputer rental fee (R) using the function R(t) where t is the number of hours youspent on the computer.Answer:​


Answer:

R = R(t)

Explanation:

R represents the rental fee, we can write it as;

R = total rent

R(t) represents the total rent multiplied by the time used in the computer shop

R(t) = rent x no. of hrs

We can safely say that the total rent is equals to the rent multiplied by the no. of hours in the computer shop. Thus,

R = R(t)

Examples:

1. A computer shop charges P15.00 every hour of use. If a player used the computer for 6 hrs. How much does the player need to pay?

Lets use the function R = R(t):

R = Total money the player need to pay

R(t) = The amount of rent per hour multiplied by the total no. of hrs used.

R = P15.00 (6 hrs)

R = P90.00

2. A taxi charges P13.00 for every kilometer traveled. If you are about to travel for 10km. How much should you pay the driver?

Lets use the function R = R(t):

R = Total fare to pay the driver

R(t) = The amount of fare per km multiplied by the total no. of km traveled.

R = P13.00 (10 km)

R = P130.00

My role as a citizen student during this pandemic

 

This pandemic makes me realize a lot of things especially to myself becoming a student and a citizen of my community. There are huge changes from being a student before to a student nowadays. With these pandemic still a threat to our health, there are restrictions and limited activities to implement by schools and lawmakers to exercise safety against this deadly airborne virus.

Schools for all levels are implementing modular learning thru online classes. Students and teachers will conduct classes using online platforms. Collaborative tools like clouds and social media is being maximized and used by every one to stay connected. This is a huge challenge to some people as not everyone can afford to buy devices and technology needed to conduct online classes. Aside from financial struggles, not all location has a stable signal. But in spite of these challenges, students like me strive hard to continue to learn and to finish my studies. My role as a student and citizen in this pandemic is to become a cooperative member of the community in promoting health safety. I could justify this role by complying to laws and ordinances being implemented in our place. I must contribute what I can to minimize virus threats.

13. what is your role as a responsible citizen of your community


Community responsibilities are an individual's duties or obligations to the community and include cooperation, respect and participation. The concept goes beyond thinking and acting as individuals to common beliefs about shared interests and life. A basic community responsibility is voting in elections.

A responsible citizen has knowledge about his/her role in community, state and the world. A responsible citizen has a role in making the world a better place to live (for every components in biosphere). A responsible citizen is change agent that acts out against injustice in social, economic, and environmental sectors.

Citizenship is the duty and responsibility as a member of a community and social responsibility is the duty of organisations in which they are held accountable for fulfilling their civic duty.

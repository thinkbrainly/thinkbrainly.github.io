Answer:  Construct a building to be rented out to others as a dormitory

Explanation:  Since Eison is nearing his retirement, he needs to invest in something that is more predictable.  The stock market may be a good investment option if Eison has a lot of time before his retirement. The challenge with the stock market is that it is volatile (it moves around up or down a lot) over a short period of time.  On the other hand, renting out a dormitory where Eison knows there is good demand may be a safer bet.  This way he will be able to earn monthly rental income to pay for his expenses during retirement.

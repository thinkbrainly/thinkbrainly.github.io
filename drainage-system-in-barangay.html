10. this is the soil type best for drainage system​


Answer:

Creating Well-draining soil

Compacted, clay and sandy soils all benefit from being amended with rich organic materials. It is not enough to just add sand to clay soil to improve drainage because that can just make the soil more like concrete.

Sandy soil

Sandy soil drains more readily than other types of soil. Sand drains so quickly that extremely sandy soil needs to be watered more often because sand will not retain water long enough for plant roots to have good access to the moisture before it drains away.

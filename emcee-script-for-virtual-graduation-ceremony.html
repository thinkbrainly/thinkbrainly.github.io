2. emcee script for teacher


Answer:

Here's a sample emcee script for a teacher's recognition event:

Good evening, everyone! Welcome to this special event where we honor and recognize the hard work and dedication of our amazing teachers. My name is [Your Name], and I am honored to be your emcee for this evening.

Tonight, we gather to pay tribute to our teachers who have made a significant impact in the lives of their students. They have not only imparted knowledge and skills but also inspired and motivated their students to become better individuals.

We have with us tonight a group of outstanding teachers who have gone above and beyond their duties to provide quality education to their students. They have spent countless hours preparing lesson plans, grading papers, and providing guidance to their students. Let's give them a round of applause for their hard work and dedication!

To start off our program, I would like to invite [Name of School Principal] to say a few words.

[School Principal speaks]

Thank you, [Name of School Principal], for your inspiring words.

Now, it's time to introduce our teachers who will be recognized tonight. As I call your name, please come forward and receive your certificate of recognition.

[Call out the names of the teachers and have them come forward to receive their certificates.]

Congratulations, teachers! Your dedication and hard work have not gone unnoticed. You have touched the lives of many students and have inspired them to reach their full potential.

Now, let's hear from some of your students. We have prepared a video presentation of messages from your students, expressing their gratitude and appreciation for all that you do.

[Play the video presentation.]

Thank you, students, for sharing your heartfelt messages.

Finally, we would like to invite all the teachers to come up on stage for a group photo.

[Take a group photo.]

Once again, congratulations to our outstanding teachers! You are truly an inspiration to us all. Thank you for your unwavering commitment to education and for making a positive impact in the lives of your students.

10. Find the meaning of the following words. 1.graduation 2.certificate 3.ceremony 4.diploma 5.graduates​


Answer:

1. Graduation - An act of graduating; the state of being graduated.

2. Certificate - An official document attesting a certain fact.

3. Ceremony - A formal religious or public occasion, typically one celebrating a particular event or anniversary.

4. Diploma - A certificate awarded by an educational establishment to show that someone has successfully completed a course of study.

5. Graduates - A person who has successfully completed a course of study or training, especially a person who has been awarded an undergraduate academic degree.

25. How schools did their graduation ceremonies during the pandemic.


Answer:

This year, however, more colleges are making plans to return to in-person commencement ceremonies, with social distancing guidelines and COVID-19 safety protocols in place. Former university president and Forbes contributor Michael T. Nietzel calls it "the year of hybridized commencements."

Institutions arranging for in-person graduation ceremonies include Brown University, which will restrict family members and friends to virtual attendance, and Bowdoin College, which will let each graduating student bring a maximum of two guests. Other schools, like the University of North Texas, are even giving 2020 grads the chance to get in on the action.

Despite improvements in COVID-19 testing and the emergence of several vaccines, a large number of schools intend to keep commencement celebrations 100% virtual for a second year in a row.

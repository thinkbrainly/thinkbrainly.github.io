20. This improves the efficiency of the kitchena. FIFOb. FIFAc. FIDOd. FIDA​


FIFO improves the efficiency of the kitchen.

Food Storage in kitchen

Storage of food, especially cooked food, which is perishable must be done properly. In order to be consumed safely, in addition to implementing FIFO, pay attention to the following, which are adjusted to food safety standards:

Do not use the original packaging

Food that has been opened, immediately transferred to a new container. The original packaging of food may be contaminated with bacteria during the transportation process. This method also prevents cockroaches from breeding. In addition, do not use reusable food packaging containers even though the containers look attractive and sturdy.

Implement a FIFO system

Use a First in First out (FIFO) stock rotation system. To make this system easier to apply, place old food on the front shelf while new food enters at the back. You can easily retrieve old food without having to remove all the contents of the shelves, especially in the refrigerator.

Separate raw and cooked food

To prevent food-to-food contamination, store raw meat and seafood separately from cooked and ready-to-eat foods. Position cooked food over raw food. Cover cooked and raw food at all times to prevent contamination between foods and so that microbacteria do not develop in the presence of oxygen.

Cool food before storing

Refrigerate cooked food before freezing so that moisture doesn't get in the container.

Put food in the kitchen

Keep toxic or chemical products away from food storage areas to prevent chemical poisoning. Materials should be placed 6 inches from the floor. Never place materials directly on the floor. For dry food, keep the temperature between 10-21 degrees Celsius to prevent moisture.

23. If you see a message to press any key to boot the CD and you fail to do, what do you think will happen? Will the computer will restart?


Answer:

If you failed to press any key from the prompt "Press any key to boot the CD" the computer will not restart but it will not carry on to its installation process. If you don't have any OS installed on your computer, you will get the message repeatedly. The “Press any key message” will appear once you set your boot drive priority on your BIOS setting.  

Typically here are the steps to enter the BIOS on windows:

Restart your PC.Press F2 or Del after the start-up screen appears.On the BIOS screen, change your boot sequence in the BIOS Setup Utility Menu.After changing the boot order you can now press “Save and Exit” key.

If your installation is done but you still get the "Press any key" message, you just have to remove your USB stick, CD, DVD, or any external device. You can also change your boot priority. However, if your computer hangs at the "Press any key to boot from CD" screen just try to unplug and plug your installation devices.

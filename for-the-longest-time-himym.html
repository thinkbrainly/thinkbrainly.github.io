How much time do people spend on social media?

As of 2019 and 2020, the average daily social media usage of internet users worldwide amounted to 145 minutes per day, up from 142 minutes in the previous year. Global impact of social media

Social media has a wide-reaching and significant impact on not only online activities but also offline behavior and life in general.

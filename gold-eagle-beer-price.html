3. how did the de beers cartel try to maintain control of the price in the diamond market __________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________​


Answer:

The De Beers cartel tried to maintain control of the price in the diamond market by using various tactics such as:

1. Controlling the supply: The De Beers cartel controlled the supply of diamonds in the market by buying diamonds from other mines and stockpiling them. This helped them regulate the quantity of diamonds in the market and maintain prices.

2. Creating an illusion of scarcity: The De Beers cartel also strategically released diamonds into the market to create an illusion of scarcity. This created a perception that diamonds were rare and valuable, and people were willing to pay more for them.

3. Marketing: The De Beers cartel invested heavily in marketing to create a demand for diamonds. They created the famous slogan "A diamond is forever" to convince people that a diamond was a timeless and valuable investment.

4. Controlling distribution: The De Beers cartel also controlled the distribution of diamonds by only allowing authorized dealers to sell diamonds. This helped them control the supply chain and maintain prices.

5. Price fixing: The De Beers cartel also engaged in price fixing by setting a minimum price for diamonds. This prevented competition and ensured that prices remained stable.


4. a conical beer glass is 18cm tall and a radius of 2cm.What is the amount of beer that the glass can hold​


Answer:

75.36 cm³

Step-by-step explanation:

The amount of the beer that the glass can hold equals the volume of the conical beer. Since the beer is a conical beer, it is a cone, so using the formula for the volume of a cone is appropriate here.

Formula for volume of cone: V = 1/3(πr²)

Plug in the given values:

V = 1/3(π)(18 cm)(2 cm)²

V = 1/3(3.14)(18 cm)(4 cm²)

V = (3.14)(6 cm)(4 cm²) = 75.36  cm³

The conical beer can hold 75.36 cm³ amount of beer.

17. The index of refraction of beer is about 1.35. what is the speed of light in beer


Solution:

The index of refraction of light in a medium is

n = c / v

where:

n = index of refraction

v = speed of light in a medium

c = speed of light in a vacuum

c = 3.00 × 10⁸ m/s

Listing the given value

n = 1.35

Solving for v

n = c / v

1.35 = 3.00 × 10⁸ m/s / v

v = 3.00 × 10⁸ m/s / 1.35

v = 2.22 × 10⁸ m/s

Therefore, the speed of light in beer is 2.22 × 10⁸ m/s.

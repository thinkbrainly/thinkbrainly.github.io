16. 1. Snow White and the Prince live happily ever after ​


Answer:

In the story of "Snow White," it is often said that Snow White and the Prince live happily ever after, meaning that they go on to lead a joyful and content life together. This phrase is commonly used in fairy tales to signify a satisfying conclusion to a story, where the characters overcome the obstacles and challenges they face and find true love and happiness. In the case of Snow White and the Prince, they are able to overcome the evil schemes of the Queen and find each other. They may be married or simply living together, but the idea is that they are able to find love and happiness in each other's company for the rest of their lives. The phrase "happily ever after" gives the story a sense of completeness and closure, with the characters' struggles resolved and a happy ending in sight

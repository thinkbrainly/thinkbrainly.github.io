8. tagpuan ng hello love goodbye


Kasagutan:

Hello Love Goodbye

Ang tagpuan ng pelikula ay sa bansang Hongkong. Tungkol ito sa isang bartender at isang domestic helper na nagmamahalan.

Si Joy ay isang OFW na walang humpay na nagsusumikap sa Hong Kong upang may maibigay sa kanyang pamilya sa Pilipinas na pera. Nais niyang lumipat sa Canada. Si Ethan ay nagtatrabaho bilang isang bartender habang hinihintay niya ang kanyang citizenship. Si Joy ay umiiwas sa mga relasyon, ngunit hindi niya napigilang mahulog kay Ethan. Noong nahulog silang dalawa sa pag-ibig, pareho silang nilang alam na pansamantala ang relasyon. Ngunit habang lumalalim ang kanilang pag-iibigan ay nahirapan na silang bumitaw.

30. what is the introduction of the movie hello love goodbye​


Answer:

Hello, Love, Goodbye is a 2019 romantic drama film directed by Cathy Garcia-Molina and produced by Star Cinema. The movie features Kathryn Bernardo as Joy and Alden Richards as Ethan, two overseas Filipino workers in Hong Kong who fall in love despite their different goals and backgrounds.

The film starts with Joy arriving in Hong Kong to work as a domestic helper, leaving behind her family and boyfriend in the Philippines. She meets Ethan, a bartender who dreams of opening his own restaurant someday. Despite their initial disagreements, they soon develop feelings for each other. However, their relationship is complicated by their respective plans for the future - Joy wants to save enough money to start her own business back home, while Ethan is torn between pursuing his dream and taking care of his sick mother.

As Joy and Ethan's relationship deepens, they are forced to confront the challenges of their situation and make difficult decisions about their future together. The movie explores themes of love, sacrifice, and the struggles faced by Filipino migrant workers abroad.

"Hello, Love, Goodbye" is a 2019 Filipino romantic drama film directed by Cathy Garcia-Molina. The film stars Kathryn Bernardo and Alden Richards in the lead roles and tells the story of two overseas Filipino workers (OFWs) who meet in Hong Kong and fall in love.

Joy (Kathryn Bernardo) is a domestic helper who dreams of moving to Canada to start a new life. She meets Ethan (Alden Richards), a bartender who is also an OFW, and the two develop a connection despite their busy work schedules. As they spend more time together, they begin to question their priorities and whether their careers are worth sacrificing their personal lives for.

The film explores the challenges and sacrifices that OFWs face while working abroad, as well as the joys and sorrows of falling in love in a foreign land. It also delves into themes such as family, friendship, and the pursuit of happiness. With its captivating story and heartfelt performances, "Hello, Love, Goodbye" has become a beloved Filipino film that has touched the hearts of audiences both in the Philippines and abroad.

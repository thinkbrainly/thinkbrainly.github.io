3. The table below shows a pattern of number. Column1 Column2 Column3 Column4Row1 2 4 6 8Row2 10 12 14 16Row3 18 20 22 24Row4Row5Row6Row7In which column and row will the number 222 appear? ​


Answer:

The number 222 will appear in Column 4, Row 7.

Step-by-step explanation:

The pattern shows that the sequence of numbers in each column and row is increasing by 2. Therefore, the number 222 will appear in Column 4, Row 7 since the sequence of numbers in Column 4 is 8, 10, 12, 14, 16, 18, 20, 22, 24 and 222 is two more than the number in Row 6 (220).

15. what is piano music and program music​


Answer:

program music=

Program music, instrumental music that carries some extramusical meaning, some “program” of literary idea, legend, scenic description, or personal drama. It is contrasted with so-called absolute, or abstract, music, in which artistic interest is supposedly confined to abstract constructions in sound.

piano music - sheet music to be played on a piano. sheet music - a musical composition in printed or written form; "she turned the pages of the music as he played" 2. piano music - the sound of music produced by a piano; "he thought he heard piano music next door"

Answer:

Piano music is a type of music composed for the piano. During the Romantic Period, composers broke away from rigid forms and explored more emotional themes, often using free forms like fantasy, rhapsody, ballade, and nocturne. However, not all Romantic Period piano music is in free form. Piano music continues to be a diverse and evolving genre.

4. Activity 1. Unscramble Me!Direction: Unscramble the words to show the levels of organization.1. REBISPOHE2. ONPOLATIPU3. MUNCUMITY4. MESYCOSTE5. MISNGAOR​


LEVELS OF ECOLOGICAL ORGANIZATION

Answers with Explanation:

1. BIOSPHERE

- The biosphere is the part of Earth where organisms thrive. This is the part of the planet where living things occupy and interact with one another, with nonliving things and the environment.

2. POPULATION

- The population is a group of same species of organisms. For example, a population of emperor penguins.

3. COMMUNITY

- The community is an ecological level where different species or population of organisms interact with one another.

4. ECOSYSTEM

- The ecosystem is an ecological level of organization where various species of organisms (plants and animals) live with abiotic factors which they need in their everyday activities.

5. ORGANISM

- The organism is a level of ecological organization that refers to living things. This is consisted of different organ systems and has an ability of body autonomy.

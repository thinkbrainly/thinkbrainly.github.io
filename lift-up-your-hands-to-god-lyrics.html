1. Your left hand lifts a 5 N apple by 1.8 m and your right hand lifts an 8 N grapefruit  by 0.5 m.

Left Hand                                         Right Hand

W = F × d                                     W = F × d

W = 5 N × 1.8 m                           W = 8 N × 0.5 m

W = 9 J                                         W = 4 J

a. Which hand exerted the greater force? How much?

Left hand exert 5 N force 1 N higher compared to the right hand

b. Which hand did more work? How much work was done?

Left hand did more work which is 9 J. It is 5 J higher compared to right hand which only have 4 J of work.

Power computation

Left Hand                            Right Hand

P = 10 watts                          P =  6.67 watts

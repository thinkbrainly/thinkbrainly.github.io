1. (ASAP)Given LM = 11x + 4, SE = 20x - 5, SL= 9What is SL?what is SE?What is the area of rectangle Smell?​


Answer:

SI = 15/2

LE = 12

SMEL = 108

Step-by-step explanation:

Let LM = SE, we can get 11x + 4 = 20x - 5

9x = 9

x = 1

So we can get LM = 11 × 1 + 4 = 15

SI = LM = 15/2

LE = √15² - 9² = 12

SMEL = 12 × 9 = 108

So the result is SI = 15/2, LE = 12, SMEL = 108

1. In a batch of 300 students, 73 aremembers of the Sports Club, 220 aremembers of the Music Club, and 70students are in both clubs. How manystudents are:a. in either the Sports Club or MusicClub?b. in neither of the clubs?​


Students (U) = 300

Let, S = sports, M = music

n(S) = 73

n(M) = 220

n(S) ∩ n(M) = 70

To solve for the number of students who are into sports club only:

n(S) = 73

73 - 70 = 3

To solve for the number of students who are into music club only:

n(M) = 220

220-70 = 150

To solve for the number of students who are in neither of the clubs:

300-(30+70+150) = 77

7. Josephine's sport shop gives a discount of 10% off the list price of all sports equipment to members of the sports club. What discount would a club member get on a tennis racket with a list price of Php 7 835.00?​


Answer:

A club member would get a discount of:

10% of Php 7 835.00 = (10/100) x Php 7 835.00 = Php 783.50

Therefore, the discount a club member would get on a tennis racket with a list price of Php 7 835.00 is Php 783.50.

The discounted price would be:

Php 7 835.00 - Php 783.50 = Php 7 051.50

So, the club member would pay Php 7 051.50 for the tennis racket after the 10% discount.

To obtain the number of students who are members of Drama Club only, simply subtract 40 from 170, and we will get 130.

16. 40 out of the 60 members of a sports club are boys. What percent of the club members are girls?


Given:
           60 = the total number of members in the sports club
           40 = number of boys of the club

Find:  Percent of the club that are girls.

Solution:
              There are 60 total members, 40 boys, so the number of girls is obviously 20, because 60 - 40 = 20
  
             This time we must find the percent of the club that are girls.
This also means - what percent of 60 is 20.

Percentage ÷ Base = Rate
20    ÷    60   = 0.33333...
Now, change  0.33333... to percent by multiplying by 100 and inserting the percent symbol

0.33333...  x 100 =      33. 333333......% or just 33.33%

Final Answer: 
                          33.33%

28. two seventh of the sports club members are girls.If there are 84 members of the club, how many are boys?​


If two sevenths of the sports club members are girls, then the remaining members, which represent five sevenths of the total, must be boys.

Let's use algebra to solve for the number of boys (B):

27. the weight of your mapeh teacher is 350N.find his or her mass??​


The answer is 35.714 kg

Because we’re using the formula to find mass which is:

m = w/g (where w is weight and g is for gravitational speed)

Since Earth’s gravitational speed is 9.8 m/s squared. We can now solve the problem.

Substitute its variables.

m = 350N/9.8m/s^2

Solve it and it will become 35.714 kg.

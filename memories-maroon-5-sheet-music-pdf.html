Characteristics of Red Color

History says, for thousands of years of human civilization, the color red evokes emotions and makes a person spend more money.

The ancient Greek civilization used the color red to represent the god of war, meaning strength and power. Also, the color red symbolizes masculinity for their culture.

Chinese culture assumes that the color red brings good luck or hockey. The bride and groom will wear red with the hope that their life after marriage will be filled with prosperity and wealth.

The time during The New Society (1972-1980)

The New Society period began on September 21, 1972. The annual Carlos Planca awards continue to be presented. The New Society attempted to prohibit indecency and other publications that had an adverse impact on people's morals. As a result, all school publications were temporarily suspended, as were all student clubs.

The Carlos Palanca Awards continued to be presented annually. Nearly all themes in the majority of publications concerned the nation's development or progress, such as the Green Revolution, family planning, good nutrition, the environment, and pollution.

Essays, discussions, and poetry were the dominant types of literature at this time. The short stories, along with the novels and plays, were written in the same style as before the advent of activism.

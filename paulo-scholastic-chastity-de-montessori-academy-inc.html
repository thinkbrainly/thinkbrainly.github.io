29. he founded the centro escolar de senoritas conservatory of music & buencamino music academy in 1930


He founded the centro escolar de senoritas conservatory of music & buencamino music academy in 1930

His name was Francisco Beltran Buencamino Sr Francisco Beltran Buencamino  Bulacan, at San Miguel de Mayumo, 5 November 1883. He is Fortunato Buencamino's sixth  of ten children, and Luisa Beltran.  His father was a master church organist  and band, and his mother was a singer.  Francisco was married to Pilar Luceno, and they  had two children who took up music as well.  Francisco learnt his  father's music first.  He could play the organ at the age of 12. At  14 he was sent to the Liceo de Manila to study.  There, under Marcelo Adonay he  took lessons in composition and harmony. He also took lessons in pianoforte  under a professor of Spanish guitar.  As he was involved in the  sarswela he did not finish his schooling.  Francisco Buencamino teached music at the Ateneo de Manila and  at the Centro Escolar de Senoritas in the early 1900s.  He founded the Conservatory of Music at  the latter, and remained its head until 1938.  He also conducted music lessons at the  Liceo de Manila, at the same time.  In 1930 he founded  the Buencamino Academy of Music.

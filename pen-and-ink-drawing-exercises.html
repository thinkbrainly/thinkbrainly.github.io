8. using a venn diagram a class of 40 grade 7 students were asked whether they use black or blue ink pen or both. 28 use black ink pens and 17 use blue ink pens. how many use both ink color?​


Answer:

5 students

Step-by-step explanation:

Total number of students = 40

Using a Venn diagram, 28 students use black and 17 use blue...

We have...

black = 28

blue = 17

(Some students use both ink color.)

Total the number of responses...

28 + 17 = 45

There are a total of 45 responses but there are only 40 students. Therefore, some of them answered both black and blue.

45 - 40 = 5

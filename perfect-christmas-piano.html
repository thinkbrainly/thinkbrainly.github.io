11. What one word brings meaning to the lines, "Ebony and Ivory, live together in perfect harmony, Side by side on my piano, keyboard, Oh Lord, Why don't we?"


One word that brings meaning to the lines mentioned above, or perhaps the whole song is the word harmony.

Ebony represents the black keys in a piano keyboard while the Ivory represents the white keys. Although they're different in color, they work together to create that perfect harmony in music.

Just like in our society, we may all be different physically and culturally but we may work together and accept one another in order to achieve that perfect harmony and make this world a peaceful and better place.

No discrimination, just peace and harmony.

Answer:

At the simplest level, the song is about the ebony (black) and ivory (white) keys on a piano, but also deals with integration and racial harmony on a deeper level. The title was inspired by McCartney hearing Spike Milligan say "black notes, white notes, and you need to play the two to make harmony, folks!"

Ang Pasko ay isang maligayang selebrasyon na ginaganap sa buong mundo tuwing ika-25 ng Disyembre. Ito ay isang panahon ng kasiyahan, pagpapakita ng pagmamahal, at pagbibigay ng mga regalo sa pamilya at mga mahal sa buhay.

Sa panahon ng Pasko, maraming tao ay nagsasagawa ng mga tradisyunal na gawain tulad ng paglalagay ng mga decorasyon sa bahay at sa mga hardin, pagluluto ng mga masasarap na pagkain, at pagpapakain sa mga nangangailangan. Sa maraming bahagi ng mundo, ang Pasko ay isang mahalagang panahon ng taon, na kung saan maraming tao ang nakikipag-ugnayan sa kanilang mga mahal sa buhay na hindi sila nakakakita sa regular na panahon.

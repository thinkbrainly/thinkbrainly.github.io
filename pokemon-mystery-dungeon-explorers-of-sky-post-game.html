24. What are the skills and attitude needed to solve the murder mystery game?


#BrainlyFast

The game is like a puzzle to solve who is the killer. The player will act being a detective and find out who committed the crime.

Keen observer

You should be able to observe carefully the surrounding for some evidence that will help you solve the crime. You look for some evidences like the weapon, the blood and possible traces for you to identify the suspect.

Critical Thinker

You should also be a critical thinker. Analyzing the evidence, the situation, the surrounding will help you figure out who is the suspect. One wrong decision will lead to wrong accusation and you failed the game.

Problem solver

You should also be a problem solver who will not get tired in solving the case and find out who the suspect is.

For me this three are the most important skills and attitude needed to solve the murder mystery game.

Answer: 1 11/12 kg of pork

Step-by-step explanation: First add 9 3/4 and 3 1/3:

9 + 3 = 12

Second, find the LCD of 3/4 and 1/3 and rewrite to solve with the equivalent fractions.

LCD = 12

9/12 + 4 1/12 = 13/12 or 1 1/12

12 + 1 1/12 = 13 1/12, then substract it to the total weight of pork.

15 - 13 1/12, solve the whole number parts.

15 - 13 = 2, then subtract the fraction on it.

2 - 1/12 = 1 11/12

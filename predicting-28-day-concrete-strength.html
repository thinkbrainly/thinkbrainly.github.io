18. 28.The area of the concrete rectangular pathway is 300 m2 and it is perimeter pathway is 70 m. what is the length of the pathway?​


Answer:

The length of the concrete rectangular pathway is 20m.

Step-by-step explanation:

Ang product po ng length at width ng rectangular pathway ay magrerepresent po ng area niya, which is 300m². Hence, length (l) times width (w) = 300 or lw = 300.

Also, twice po ng sum of the length at width ng pathway gives the perimeter, which is 70m. Hence, 2l + 2w = 70. Divide po both sides by 2, if possible, then magiging l + w = 35 na po siya.

By inspection po, the numbers whose product is 300 and whose sum is 35 are 20 and 15.

Product: 20 • 15 = 300

Sum: 20 + 15 = 35

So ang length po ay 20m at ang width ay 15m.

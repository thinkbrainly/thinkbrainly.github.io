19. how to do thesis statement and script on market research?​


Answer in explanation:

To write a thesis statement start by asking a question about the topic-- in this case market research--.

Next, write down/note the answer in order to start including more reasons why the answer is the answer.

And then polish it to get a more detailed thesis statement.

The thesis statement you end up with should give you a good idea of what to write next; you can brainstorm more ideas for an outline or start writing for a less organized draft before looking over it and finishing up.

A thesis statement is a crucial part of any research paper. It is the central argument or claim that the paper will be defended. This statement should be clear and concise, as it will serve as a road map for the rest of the paper. The thesis statement will also help to keep the paper focused and on track.

16. Leah is 2 less than 3 times Rachel's Age. 3 years from now, Leah will be 7 more than twice Rachel's Age. How old will Rachel be in 3 years from now?​


Answer:

Rachel will be 15 years old in 3 years.

Step-by-step explanation:

Present ages:

Rachel: xLeah: 3x - 2

Ages 3 years:

Rachel: x + 3Leah: 3x - 2 + 3 or  3x + 1

Condition:  3 years from now, Leah will be 7 more than twice Rachel's Age.

Equation:

3x + 1 = 2(x+3) + 7

Find Rachel's present age, x:

3x + 1 = 2x + 6 + 7

3x - 2x = 6 + 7 - 1

x = 12

Rachel is 12 years old.

How old will Rachel be in 3 years from now?​

12 + 3 = 15 years old

Rachel will be 15 years old in 3 years.

Check:

Rachel's present age: 12 years old

Leah's present age: 3x-2  =  3(12)-2 = 36-2 = 34 years old

Ages in 3 years:

3 years from now, Leah will be 7 more than twice Rachel's Age.

34 + 3 = 2(12 + 3) + 7

37 = 2 (15) + 7

37 = 30 + 7

37 = 37  (True)

29. The ratio of jaydens height to Rachel's height is 5to8 if Rachel is 57 centimeters taller than jaydens find jaydens height


Answer:

The ratio of jaydens height to Rachel's height is 5to8 if Rachel is 57 centimeters taller than jaydens find jaydens height.

Jayden is 95 centimeters tall.

Step-by-step explanation:

What is asked?

Find jaydens height.

What are given?

ratio of Jayden's height to Rachel's - 5:8

Rachel's height - 57 cm taller than Jayden's

Let us start finding Jayden's height. Based on the problem above, the ratio of their heights is 5:8, meaning Rachel is taller by 3. The problem also stated that Rachel's height is 57 centimeters more than Jayden's.

Solution:

57 ÷ 3 = 19

Rachel's height

8 × 19 = 152 cm

Jayden's height

5 × 19 = 95 cm

Final Answer:

Jayden's height is 95 cm.

Checking:

Let us check if our answers are correct.

152 cm - 95 cm = 57 cm ✔

Our answers are correct because their heights difference is 57 cm.

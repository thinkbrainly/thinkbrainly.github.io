2. explain the process of split second action and reaction.​


Explanation:

Split second action and reaction is a process in which two events occur almost simultaneously. It is a phenomenon that occurs when an action triggers an immediate response from another person or object. For example, when someone throws a ball, the person catching it must react quickly to catch it before it hits the ground. This requires split second action and reaction as both parties must act and react at the same time in order for the event to be successful.

29. in movie maker, it stop playing the music at a point in time in to the movie.a. fadein/fadeout ta commandb. split tab commandc. trim tab command​


Answer:

B.

Explanation:

. Add video file.

2. On Home tab use Add Music to add music to the whole clip.

3. Move the vertical cursor on the movie to where you want the music to stop.

4. On Edit tab select Split.

5. Now using mouse cursor select just the audio that runs along the top of the video AFTER your split point.

6. Hit Delete on your keyboard.

You could also do it by reducing the volume of the section of audio after the split to zero.

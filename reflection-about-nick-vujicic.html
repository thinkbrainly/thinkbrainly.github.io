13. 1. Describe Nick Vujicic. What is very unique about him?​


Answer:

Who is Nick Vujicic?

Nick Vujicic (pronounced "voo-yi-chich") is an Australian-American speaker, New York Times best-selling book, coach, and businessman who was born without arms or legs.

Nick had to overcome numerous challenges in his life, including living without limbs, being harassed at school, and fearing for his future with no clear purpose insight. His emotions of powerlessness and isolation, coupled with a lack of hope, prompted him to try suicide.

Nick overcame adversity and discovered essential principles that enabled him to identify his mission and turn challenges into opportunities, making him one of the world's most sought-after keynote speakers! Nick's inspirational lectures and dynamic coaching have given millions of individuals hope, purpose, and the strength to overcome their obstacles.

Nick is an anti-bullying ambassador who speaks to millions of students throughout the world in collaboration with various governments' education departments. Nick has developed a unique Social Emotional Learning Curriculum called "Attitude Is Altitude," in which he teaches kids how to make good life transformations.

He is also the founder and CEO of Life Without Limbs, a non-profit organization.

Nick has appeared on the BBC, 60 Minutes Australia, CBS Sunday Morning, Oprah's LifeClass, USA Today, People Magazine, ABC News, TLC, LA Stories, and many other shows. Through "The Nick Vujicic Podcast," he directly interacts with his large social media presence and connects them through webinars and counseling.

Nick's enthusiasm is to encourage and empower the world to know that we can all rise above adversity and overcome any impairment of the heart and mind! Husband to his beautiful wife Kanae and father of four children, Nick's passion is to inspire and equip the world to know that we can all rise above hardship and conquer any disability of the soul and spirit!

This charismatic evangelist has achieved more in his lifetime than most people do in a lifetime. He is a writer, musician, and actor who enjoys fishing, painting, and swimming. Vujicic, who has traveled across the world telling his experience with millions around the world, thanked God for delivering him from the two greatest impairments in life: sin and death.

22. how to response the request letter​


Think of the situation for which you are responding. Write a response accordingly with a perfect analysis of the letter.

If the letter is formal, make sure you write it in a perfect format. Keep your tone positive and professional. It would require a good selection of words to attract someone.

In case it is a letter to clear some misunderstanding, keep your tone positive always. It is to ensure it does not raise further disputes.

Do not always try adding your point of view. First, read what others are saying and try to analyse if they think better than you.

Do not take excess time in responding to someone. The sender generally looks for quick replies from you.

Your letter must be precise and straightforward so that receiver understands it quickly.

Suppose someone is asking for your help, but you are unable to do so. You must show regret behaviour and mention you are unable to help them.

If some response letters need a negative response, make sure it should not offend the receiver.

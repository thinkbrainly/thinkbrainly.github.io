A Venn diagram, also called primary diagram, set diagram or logic diagram, is a diagram that shows all possible logical relations between a finite collection of different sets.

Answer:

A Venn diagram is an illustration that uses circles to show the relationships among things or finite groups of things. Circles that overlap have a commonality while circles that do not overlap do not share those traits. Venn diagrams help to visually represent the similarities and differences between two concepts

28. Ano ang ibig sabihin ng "Sabihin mo sakin kung sino-sino ang kaibigan mo at sasabihin ko sayo kung sino ka"


Answer:

Sabin hin molang Wala namang masama

Ang ibig sabihin ng "Sabihin mo sakin kung sino-sino ang kaibigan mo at sasabihin ko sayo kung sino ka" ay isang makasariling pahayag, sapagkat gusto niyang alamin muna ang mga kaibigan ng tao para malaman kung kasama siya doon, at kapag hindi, maaaring mga masasamang katangian ang sasabihin nya tungkol sa 'kung sino ka'.

(P.S hindi lahat ng nagsasabi nito ay makasarili, opinyon ko lamang ito, maaaring naguguluhan o confused lamang ang tao)

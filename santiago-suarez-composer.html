21. narrate the life and work of santiago suarez​


Answer:

SANTIAGO SUAREZ

One of the most prolific Filipino musical composers, Santiago S. Suarez was born on May 23, 1901, in Sampaloc, Manila.

Suarez studied at the Ateneo de Manila in Intramuros. He took up music at the University of the Philippines and trained under the noted musicians like Cayetano Jacobe, Pedro Eloriaga and Nicanor Abelardo.

He composed more than 400 pieces of harana, balitaw, danzas and ballads.

Among his compositions:

Bakya Mo Neneng

Neneng’s Wooden Clogs

Sa Libis ng Nayon

In the Meadow

Caprichosa

Capricious Woman

Tapis Mo, Inday

Your Overskirt, Inday

Bulaklak at Paruparo

Flower and Butterfly

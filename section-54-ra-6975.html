5. Ra 6713 section 14 paki elaborate pls​


Answer:

Section 14 of RA 6713 is a rule that public officials and employees in the Philippines should follow. It says that they cannot work in their profession (like being a lawyer, doctor, or accountant) while working for the government, unless they are authorized by the Constitution or the law. This is to avoid situations where their private interests could conflict with their responsibilities as public officials. For example, if a government official who is also a lawyer represents a client who is involved in a case that the government is handling, this could create a conflict of interest. By following this rule, public officials and employees are expected to be fair and impartial in their work, and to prioritize their duties as government servants over their private interests.

22. Section 13 -Implementing rules and Regulations RA 9105​


Explanation:

The implementing rules and regulations of RA 9105 shall be promulgated by the Department of the Interior and Local Government (DILG), the National Police Commission (NAPOLCOM), and other concerned government agencies, after due consultation with the local government units (LGUs) concerned and other stakeholders. The implementing rules and regulations shall provide for measures to ensure the efficient and effective implementation of the Anti-Terrorism Act and shall include, but shall not be limited to, the following:

1. The creation of a National Anti-Terrorism Council (NATC), headed by the Executive Secretary, to coordinate and monitor the implementation of the Act.

2. The establishment of a fund for the implementation of the Act and for providing assistance to the families of victims of terrorism.

3. The issuance of appropriate identification cards to members of authorized law enforcement and security units that are engaged in anti-terrorism activities.

4. The adoption of appropriate measures to protect the rights of victims of terrorism and their families.

5. The adoption of appropriate measures to ensure the speedy and fair trial of persons charged with terrorism or any crime related thereto.

6. The establishment of a system of rewards for persons providing information that would lead to the arrest of terrorists or their conviction.

7. The adoption of measures to ensure the effective monitoring of the activities of suspected terrorists.

8. The adoption of measures to enhance the capacity of LGUs in the prevention of terrorism and the protection of their constituents.

9. The adoption of appropriate measures to ensure the protection of media practitioners and journalists who are covering terrorism-related activities.

10. The adoption of measures to ensure the protection of the rights of persons within the custody of the police or military, who are suspected of involvement in terrorism-related activities.

30. define section 10 of RA 10173

RA 10173

Explanation:

Republic Act No. 10173 or the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (DPA) “to protect the fundamental human right to privacy of communication while ensuring free flow of information to promote innovation and growth [and] the [State’s] inherent obligation to ensure that personal information in information and communications systems in government and in the private sector are secured and protected”.

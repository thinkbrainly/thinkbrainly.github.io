1. The periodic table contains information about elements, such as their symbol, atomic number, and atomic mass.

2. Color is an element consisting of hues, of which there are three properties: hue, chroma or intensity, and value. Color is present when light strikes an object and it is reflected back into the eye, a reaction to a hue arising in the optic nerve.

5. The least reactive elements are

the noble gases. Within this group, helium is the least reactive element, forming no stable compounds.

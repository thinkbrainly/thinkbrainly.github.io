16. sketcha figure and solvr each right triangle angle at c, given that: 1. a= 15.8 b= 21​


Answer:

Given that its a Right triangle,

and the following are given:

a = 15.8

b = 21

The required side is:

c = ?

According to the pythagorean theorem:

When a triangle has a right angle (90°) and squares are made on each of the three sides, then the biggest square has the exact same area as the other two squares put together!

https://brainly.ph/question/77971

Hence,

a² + b² = c²

In this problem to find the value of the hypotenuse "c" we will be needing to  equate the pythagorean formula to c:

Hence,

c = √ (a² + b²)

c = √ (15.8² + 21²)

c = 26.28

25. list 5 Philippine government / agency that involved in the communicable diseases ​


Answer:

1.Department of Health (DOH) - The DOH is the primary agency of the Philippine government responsible for the promotion and protection of the health of the Filipino people. It plays a key role in the prevention and control of communicable diseases through programs such as immunization, disease surveillance, and outbreak response.

2.Research Institute for Tropical Medicine (RITM) - The RITM is a research and training center under the DOH that focuses on tropical and infectious diseases. It provides laboratory support for the diagnosis and surveillance of communicable diseases, including COVID-19, HIV/AIDS, and tuberculosis.

3.National Epidemiology Center (NEC) - The NEC is a division of the DOH responsible for disease surveillance and epidemiological investigation. It collects and analyzes data on communicable diseases to support public health decision-making and response.

4.Philippine Red Cross (PRC) - The PRC is a humanitarian organization that provides disaster response, health services, and blood services. It plays a key role in the prevention and control of communicable diseases through programs such as blood safety, community health, and disaster response.

5.Philippine National Police (PNP) - The PNP is the national police force of the Philippines, responsible for law enforcement and public safety. It plays a role in the prevention and control of communicable diseases by enforcing quarantine measures and ensuring compliance with public health regulations during outbreaks.

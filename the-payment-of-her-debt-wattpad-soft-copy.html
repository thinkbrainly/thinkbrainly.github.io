23. What is the rate of interest charged on a P12,000 loan if the debt is settled by a payment of P13,200 at the end of 2 years?​


We can use the formula for simple interest to solve for the rate:

I = P * r * t

Where:

I = interest

P = principal or amount of loan

r = rate of interest per year

t = time in years

We know that the principal (P) is P12,000, the payment (amount due) is P13,200, and the time (t) is 2 years. We can solve for the interest (I) by subtracting the principal from the payment:

I = amount due - principal

I = P13,200 - P12,000

I = P1,200

Now, we can solve for the rate (r):

r = I / (P * t)

r = P1,200 / (P12,000 * 2)

r = 0.05 or 5%

Therefore, the rate of interest charged on the P12,000 loan is 5%.

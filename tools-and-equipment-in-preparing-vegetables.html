Land Preparation is the term use on how farmers prepare their field before planting.

Explanation:

Farmer of Mustard: commonly on preparation of planting vegetables like mustard have two methods the first one is direct seeding whereas the seeds where broadcast on the bed, on the preparation of the plot the common tools the is used are pick mattock, grab hoe, and rake if the area is already cleared of weeds, once the are have weeds before making plot it need to be cleared using bolo knife. The other methods is transplanting wherein you have seedlings to be transplanted on the plot or bed.

Farmer of rice: on preparation of paddy rice fields the required equipments are; hand tractor or four wheeled tractor, implements like plow and harrow are also needed. This is commonly applice on hectares of rice paddy fields.

Farmer of squash: preparing the land for squash needs equipment like four wheeled tractor or hand tractor, grab hoe for making holes on direct seeding.

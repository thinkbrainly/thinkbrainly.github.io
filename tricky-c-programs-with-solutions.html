9. Here is a tricky but fun problem involving large exponents or high powers. Which of the following numbers is greater, 31¹³ or 17¹⁷? Please answer with solution, thanks in advance! :))


Given :31¹³17¹⁷To Find :Which of the following numbers is greater, 31¹³ or 17¹⁷ ? Solution :

First of all you need to notice that,

31 + 1 = 32 = [tex]\sf 2^5[/tex]17 - 1 = 16 = [tex]\sf 2^4[/tex]Now,

[tex]\sf 31^{13} < 32^{13}[/tex]

[tex]:\implies\sf (2^5)^{13} = 2^{65}[/tex]

[tex]:\implies\sf 2(2^4)^{16} = 2 \times 16^{16}[/tex]

[tex]\therefore\: \underline{\underline{\sf 16^{17} < 17^{17}}}[/tex]

Hence, 17¹⁷ is greater than 31¹³.

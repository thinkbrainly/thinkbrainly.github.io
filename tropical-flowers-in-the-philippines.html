16. is the philippines located in tropics zone


Yes, the Philippines is located in the tropics zone. It is situated near the equator, between 5 and 20 degrees north latitude, which makes it a tropical country. The climate in the Philippines is generally hot and humid, with temperatures that can range from 20 to 38 degrees Celsius depending on the season and location. The country experiences two main seasons: the rainy season, which lasts from June to November, and the dry season, which runs from December to May. The Philippines' tropical location has a significant impact on its flora and fauna, as well as on the daily lives of its people.

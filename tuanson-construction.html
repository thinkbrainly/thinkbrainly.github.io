CHARACTERISTICS OF THE CHANSON

CHANSONChanson means "song" in French. Chanson music encompasses many periods of French song, ranging from monophonic chant in the Middle Ages through polyphonic singing in the Renaissance. Modern chanson music bridges the gap between nineteenth-century nightclub music in Paris and modern pop music.

CHARACTERISTICS

Every chanson is monophonic. Ars nova composers created both holy and profane songs. Music has generally been unrelated to mathematics and geometry in the Western tradition. Only the melodies remain. The monophonic chansons demonstrate the evolution of complicated musico-poetic forms derived from the songs of the trouvères' slightly older predecessors, the troubadours.

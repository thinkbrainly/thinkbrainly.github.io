5. in 2017 the company's revenue 36/25 of it's 2016 revenue what is the company's revenue as a percentof its revenue in 2016


Answer:

144%

Step-by-step explanation:

Problem:

Convert 36/25 to percent.

Simply divide multiple of 100 by the denominator of the given fraction. In this problem the number is 100:

100 ÷2 5 = 4

Multiply the numerator by the result:

36 × 4 = 144

The new fraction is 144/100 or read as 144 hundredths.

Therefore:

36/25 = 144/100

144/100 = 144%

The company's revenue increased by 144% in 2017.

26. the water that freezes in cracks and forms ice pushes the rock to crack​


Answer:

Ice wedging

Water seeps into cracks in the rocks, and, as the temperature drops below freezing, the water expands as ice in the cracks. The expansion exerts tremendous pressure on the surrounding rock and acts like a wedge, making cracks wider. After repeated freezing and thawing of water, the rock breaks apart.

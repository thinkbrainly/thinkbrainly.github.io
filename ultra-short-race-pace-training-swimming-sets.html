Answer:

2 480

Step-by-step explanation:

= 480 - 80 (*each team member swims 80 meters less) = 400

= 400 x 5 (the first swimmer swims 480 m. while the 5 swimmers swim 400) = 2 000

= 2 000  + 480 (The 2 000 is the 5 swimmers' meter and the 480 is the first swimmer) = 2 480

Therefore, the whole team swam 2 480 meters in the relay race.

29. for which swimming stroke is diving not recommended to start a race? ​


Answer:

breaststroke

Explanation:

Diving is not recommended to start a race for the breaststroke swimming stroke. In breaststroke, the rules require that the swimmer's hands touch the wall simultaneously at the end of each stroke cycle. Starting with a dive can make it difficult for the swimmer to time their stroke cycle properly and touch the wall simultaneously, which can result in disqualification. Instead, swimmers in breaststroke typically begin the race from a stationary position with their hands placed on the edge of the pool. For the other three swimming strokes - freestyle, backstroke, and butterfly - diving starts are allowed and are commonly used to begin races.

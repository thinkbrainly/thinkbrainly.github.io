4. A lighthouse flashes its light every 12 minutes. Another lighthouse flashes every 15 minutes. If they flash at 1:00 pm, what time will they next flash together?​


Answer:

2:00 pm

Step-by-step explanation:

Find the LCM, prime factorization can be used,

12 = 4 • 3 = 2 • 2 • 3

15 = 5 • 3

Multiply each prime factor by itself by how much it occurs the most in any number

2 occurs 2 times in 12,

3 occurs 1 time in each number,

5 occurs one time in 15

LCM = 2 • 2 • 3 • 5

= 12 • 5

= 60

Therefore they flash together every 60 minutes or every hour.

1 hour after 1:00 pm is 2:00 pm

Depends where in the Philippines you are. Much of the country experiences heavy rains and lots of typhoons every year, so flooding is practically guaranteed.
